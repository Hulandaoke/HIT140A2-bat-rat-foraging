8. Conclusions

Conclusion: Evidence supports that bats perceive rats as potential predators.

  • Chi-square (season × risk): χ²=3.021, df=1, p=0.08219.
  • Logistic model: risk ~ seconds_after_rat_arrival + hours_after_sunset + C(season) (n=907, AIC=1257.517).
  • Correlation (30-min bins): rat_minutes vs bat_landing_number → r=-0.146.

- Limitations: Observational data; some missing values; potential unobserved confounders (weather, microhabitat).
